# KOPLO Prompt Base

## Goal

Membuat prompt AI image/video untuk karakter ultra-realistis dalam suasana dangdut koplo/Pantura yang natural.

## Core Rules

- Realism over effects
- Natural human movement
- No robotic motion
- No TikTok-style choreography
- Keep face consistency
- Keep body consistency
- Keep costume consistency
- Mostly dancing in place
- Subtle shoulder and torso groove
- Tiny foot adjustments

## Base Prompt

```text
Ultra-realistic cinematic scene of [CHARACTER] in an authentic Indonesian dangdut koplo village party environment, natural lighting, realistic human movement, relaxed friendly expression, dancing in place with subtle shoulder and torso groove, tiny foot adjustments, no exaggerated choreography, no robotic motion, consistent face, consistent body, consistent costume.
```
