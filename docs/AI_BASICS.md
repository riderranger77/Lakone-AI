# AI Basics

## What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the field of computer science focused on creating systems that can perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.

Examples include:

- ChatGPT
- Gemini
- Claude
- Grok
- DeepSeek

---

## Types of AI

### Narrow AI

Designed for specific tasks.

Examples:

- ChatGPT
- Google Translate
- Siri

---

### General AI

An AI capable of performing almost any intellectual task that humans can do.

Currently still under research.

---

## Generative AI

Generative AI creates new content such as:

- Text
- Images
- Music
- Video
- Code

Examples:

- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Gemini
- Midjourney
- Dreamina
- Kling AI

---

## Why Learn AI?

- Improve productivity
- Automate repetitive tasks
- Enhance creativity
- Solve complex problems
- Build AI-powered workflows
